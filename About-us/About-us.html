About-us

I began BudgetTravelTips when as I noticed there was not one site to provide advice on how to get the most out of your holiday. Here at BudgetTravelTips we believe that everyone deserves to have access to the same experiences and cultures regardless of income.

We provide general advice on destinations from all continents with a money-saver motive in mind, which we hope will reduce at least one barrier between venturing on you dream holiday.
